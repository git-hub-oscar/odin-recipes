Lasagna!

Description

This lasagna recipe takes a little work, but it is so satisfying and filling that it's worth it!

Homemade lasagna isn't as hard to make as it seems. Don't be intimidated by all the layers of delicious Italian flavor - try this homemade lasagna recipe today!

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Fresh parsley
  • Dried basil leaves
  • Salt
  • Italian seasoning
  • Fennel seeds
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta.
  4. Layer the lasagna:
    1. Meat sauce.
    2. Noodles.
    3. Ricotta mixture.
    4. Mozzarella slices.
    5. Meat sauce.
    6. Parmesan cheese.
    7. Repeat the layers then top with parmesan.

  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
